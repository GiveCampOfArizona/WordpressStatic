Welcome to AzGiveCamp where we code it forward to help non-profits in need.

Our next event is the Phoenix 2017 Hackathon-of-Help.  Friday, March 24th at 4pm to Sunday, March 26th at 4pm at the new Galvanize facility in Phoenix.

Are you great at developing web sites and programs? Have you ever wanted to help build your community but find yourself unsuited to putting up drywall? This is the place for you.

Are you a non-profit organization in need of digital help but can’t afford precious resources on your web presence or data base creation etc?

We are a local chapter of the national GiveCamp organization and serve the Arizona area. Over 2.5 days several teams of developers, coders and digital artists will help these groups to realize their digital goals.

You can help!!!

We need coders and sponsors to make this happen. Look around and find your fit.
