Donate

Can’t volunteer, but still want to help AZGiveCamp? Please consider sponsoring our efforts with a financial donation. Any amount will help and will be used to help fund this amazing weekend. Use the link below or Contact us to help.


— A donation of $10 will buy a meal for a volunteer
— A donation of $25 will buy snacks and beverages for a team of volunteers
— A donation of $50 or more will buy a prize to recognize a volunteer’s above-and-beyond efforts

If you would like to make a donation in honor of someone’s birthday, a holiday, or another event, please let us know via the Contact Us page.

You are also welcome to send your donation by check made payable to GiveCamp of Arizona to PO Box 63262, Phoenix AZ 85082.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact the organizers using the Contact Us page.
