Charities

Arcosante is looking for us to rig up the IoT sensors on Rasperry Pis to their backend, and build a React front end to visualize the data. Come meet Rob.

Furnishing Dignity is looking to build a system to convert their walk-through list and pick-list from printed sheets to a 2-page iPad website. Come meet Joyce.

Impact One Breast Cancer Foundation is looking to build a simple inventory tracking system to help them report on their successes to donors. Come meet Elizabeth.

Patches Animal Rescue is looking to build a system that'll help them advertise animals to attract long-term fosters from senior humans. Come meet Wayne.

Community Cause is looking to build a system for posting wish lists so donors can discover local charities. Come meet Clare.

Arizonans for Children is looking to build a check-in system that'll replace their current paper form and allow them to count visitors more quickly. Come meet Laura.
